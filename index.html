Cecilia's Art Studio

Cecilia's art studio is two spaces. The first is the storage space. This room is often messy, with many materials. There are multiple sets of plastic drawers. There are also many extra materials lying on the floor. Some of these materials have not been put back since they were last used, but the majority of them simply do not have a spot. This room also contains a bunk bed. The top bunk is used for storage, while the bottom bunk doubles as a guest bed. There are no curtains on the window, but there is a sticker.

The working space is located in what would have been the dining room. There is a long desk pushed up against the wall. There are shelves both on top of and below the desk. There is also an adjustable table easel, a standing easel, and a spare standing easel tucked behind the desk. The desk is covered in the most recently used art supplies, as well as works in progress. There are multiple seating options.
