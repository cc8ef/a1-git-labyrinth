Cecilia's Art Studio

Cecilia's art studio is divided into two spaces. The first is the storage space. This is in the smallest bedroom of the house. It is often messy, with materials organized in ways that only make sense to Cecilia. There are multiple sets of plastic drawers. There are also many disorganized materials lying in piles on the floor. Some of these materials have not been put back since the last time they were used, but the majority of them simply do not have a dedicated place. This room also contains a bunk bed. The top bunk is used for storage, while the bottom bunk is kept as a place for guests. There are no curtains on the window, but there is a butterfly sticker.

The second space is located in what would have been the dining room. There is a long desk pushed up against the wall. There are shelves both on top of and below the desk. There is also an adjustable table easel, a standing easel, and a spare standing easel tucked behind the desk. The desk is covered in the most recently used art supplies, as well as artworks in varying states of completion. There are three different seating options, depending on which Cecilia feels like using that day.
