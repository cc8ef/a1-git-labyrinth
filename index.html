Cecilia's Art Studio

Cecilia's art studio is not only one, but two spaces. The first is the storage space. This storage takes up an entire room. It is often messy, with many materials organized in ways that only make sense to Cecilia. There are multiple sets of plastic drawers, perfect for organizing. There are also many extra materials lying in piles on the floor. Some of these materials have not been put back since they were last used, but the majority of them simply do not have a dedicated place. This room also contains a bunk bed. The top bunk is used for storage, while the bottom bunk doubles as a guest bed. There are no curtains on the window, but there is a beautiful butterfly sticker.

The second space, the working space, is located in what would have been the dining room. There is a long desk pushed up against the wall. There are shelves both on top of and below the desk to hold a variety of materials. There is also an adjustable table easel, a standing easel, and a spare standing easel tucked behind the desk. The desk is covered in the most recently used art supplies, as well as many intriguing artworks. There are more seating options than one person could use.
