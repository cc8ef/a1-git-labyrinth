Cecilia's Art Studio

Cecilia's art studio is divided into two spaces! The first of these is the storage space. This is in the coziest room in the house. It's a little messy, because things are organized in ways that make sense to Cecilia. There are multiple sets of plastic drawers. There are also many inspiring materials lying in piles on the floor. Some of these materials have not been put back since the last time they were used on the latest project, but the majority of them just don't have a specific place yet. This room also contains a bunk bed. The top bunk is used for storage, while the bottom bunk is kept as a place for friends to stay. The window has a pretty butterfly sticker to catch the sunlight.

The second space is the working space, located in what could have been the dining room. There is a long desk pushed up against the wall. There are shelves both on top of and below the desk to hold Cecilia's favourite items! There is also an adjustable table easel, a standing easel, and a third easel tucked behind the desk. The desk is covered in the most recently used art supplies, as well as artworks Cecilia is excited to keep working on. There are three different seating options, depending on which makes Cecilia the happiest in the moment!
