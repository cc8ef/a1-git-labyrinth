Cecilia's Art Studio

Cecilia's art studio is at least a couple of spaces. There is a storage space... I think? It must take up an entire room. I believe it is often messy, with many materials organized in ways that I don't understand. There are certainly different organization tools.. There are also many extra materials lying in piles on the floor. Some of these materials don't have obvious spots they should be placed. This room also contains a bunk bed. The top bunk is used for storage, while the bottom bunk doubles as a guest bed. There are no curtains on the window, but there is a beautiful butterfly sticker.

The second space is located across the house. There is a long desk, surrounded by shelves, which carry commonly used supplies. There is also an adjustable table easel, a standing easel, and a spare standing easel tucked behind the desk. The desk is covered in the most recently used art supplies, as well as many artworks. There are more seating options than one person could use.
