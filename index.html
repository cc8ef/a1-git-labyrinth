Cecilia's Art Studio

Cecilia's art studio is divided into two spaces. The first is the storage space. This is in the smallest bedroom of the house. It is often messy, with materials organized in ways that only make sense to Cecilia. There are many disorganized materials lying in piles on the floor. Some of these materials have not been put back since the last time they were used, but the majority of them simply do not have a dedicated place.
